What is Estalara?

Estalara is the game-changer our industry has been waiting for. After years of watching good deals fall through because of distance, language barriers, and outdated processes, we built something that actually works for everyone involved.

For Agents

You know that feeling when you have the perfect property but the right buyer is halfway around the world? We've all been there. Estalara changes that. Instead of waiting for international buyers to magically appear, you can showcase your listings to verified investors in real-time, no matter where they are.

The livestreaming feature means you can walk potential buyers through properties just like you would with someone local - answering questions, pointing out details, building that personal connection that closes deals. And the AI lead generation? It's like having an assistant who never sleeps, matching your properties with serious investors who actually have the means and motivation to buy.

No more cold leads, no more language barriers, no more losing deals because someone couldn't travel to see the property in person. Just real connections with real buyers who are ready to invest.

For Investors

Finding the right international property used to mean expensive trips, unreliable information, and dealing with agents who might not understand your needs. Estalara fixes all of that. You can tour multiple properties from your living room, ask questions in your own language, and get straight answers from knowledgeable local agents.

The platform's AI assistant helps you understand local market conditions, legal requirements, and investment potential without the usual runaround. Every property is verified, every agent is vetted, and the whole process is transparent from start to finish.

Whether you're looking for a vacation home, rental income property, or long-term investment, you get access to opportunities worldwide with the confidence that comes from working with trusted professionals who know their markets inside and out.

Our Story

From a simple idea to a global platform transforming real estate

2023 - The Beginning

Estalara was founded with a vision to solve the complex challenges of international real estate transactions. Our founders recognized the need for a platform that could bridge the gap between global investors and local agents.

Starting with a small team in Dover, Delaware, we began developing the technology that would revolutionize how properties are bought and sold across borders.

2024 - Platform Launch

After months of development and testing, we launched the Estalara platform with core features including livestreaming, AI-powered matching, and multilingual support. The response was overwhelming.

Within the first year, we facilitated over €100 million in property transactions and built a community of over 1,000 agents and 10,000 investors from around the world.

2025 - Global Expansion

Today, Estalara operates in over 50 countries, supporting 25+ languages, and has processed more than €2.5 billion in property transactions. Our AI technology has matched thousands of investors with their ideal properties.

We're just getting started. With continuous innovation and a growing global network, we're committed to making international real estate investment accessible to everyone.
